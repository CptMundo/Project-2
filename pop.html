Rules

Title: Quick Pokémon Match Objective: Match as many pairs of Pokémon cards as you can in 60 seconds. How to Play: Flip two Pokémon cards at a time. Matched pairs earn you points and are removed. Keep going until the timer runs out. Scoring: 1 point per matched pair. Winning: Player with the most points at the end wins! Enjoy the speedy fun of Quick Pokémon Match!
